Who We Are

Snowy is a highly secure and reliable stake pool. Our team believes in the innovative future that Cardano promises and is dedicated to the longevity and health of its network and ecosystem.

Single Pool Operator

We operate a single pool. If we ever grow large enough that two would be an option, we pledge to help other single pool operators in order to prioritize decentralization over profits.

Decentralized

We choose to use on-premise servers to run our pool over Azure and AWS to increase decentralization.

Secure

Our team has an extensive background in cybersecurity and software development. We know what it takes to make a pool truly secure.

Reliable

The foundation of our knowledge system is administration. You can rest assured that our pool will continue operating day and night.

Achievements

SNOWY was selected as one of the 3 SundaeSwap PISO pools. We had over 3 million ADA delegated to our pool for the duration. We are proud to have been part of this massive win for decentralization.
